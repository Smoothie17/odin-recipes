Lasagna

One of the best donair recipes made in the West

Ingredients

  • 1.5pounds ground beef
  • 1/3 cup bread crumbs
  • 1.5 teaspoons paprika
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • ⅔ cup nonfat evaporated milk

Steps to create

      Step 1 - Preheat the oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit.
      Step 2 - Cook the noodles in boiling water for 4 minutes and drain with a colander. Run it through cold water to stop from further cooking. Set aside.
      Step 3 -In a large saucepan, mix sausage or ground beef and 1 1/12 of the marinara sauce. Let it simmer for 5 minutes.
      Step 4 - In a separate bowl, mix Ricotta, 3 1/2 cups of mozzarella, Parmigiano-Reggiano, and eggs.
      Step 5 - Grease a lasagna pan or a 9×13-inch baking dish with cooking spray.
      Step 6 - Spread a thin layer of the marinara sauce over the baking dish, about 3/4 cup. Layer 3 to 5 lasagna noodles over the sauce. The noodles should only overlap slightly with each other. Layer the cheese mixture of the noodles, followed by the meat sauce.
      Step 7 - Repeat the layers starting from the noodles to the meat sauce twice. Top the meat sauce with the remaining marinara sauce and sprinkle with the remaining mozzarella cheese.
      Step 8 - Cover the baking dish with foil and bake for 30 minutes. Remove the foil and cook for an additional 10 minutes.
      Step 9 - Let the lasagna sit for 10 minutes before slicing and serving. Enjoy!
