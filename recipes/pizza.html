Pizza

This classic pizza can be easily made at home within a 1hr time frame.

Ingredients

  • Mozza Cheese
  • Tomatoes
  • Mushrooms
  • Ham
  • Dough
  • Salt
  • Tomato Sauce

Steps to create

      Step 1 - Roll dough into circular shape, whilst adding salt
      Step 2 - Add tomato sauce, and smear all over the top
      Step 3 - Cover the sauce with Mozza cheese
      Step 4 - Add toppings
      Step 5 - Add more cheese if desired
      Step 6 - Once oven has reached 400F, cook for 20min
